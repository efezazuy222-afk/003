Wuxi Tours

Wuxi, a city in Jiangsu province, is known for its beautiful scenery, rich cultural heritage, and economic prosperity. Located on the shores of Taihu Lake, Wuxi offers stunning natural landscapes and historical attractions.

Top Attractions

  • Taihu Lake: One of China's largest freshwater lakes, offering scenic views, boat tours, and water activities.
  • Lingshan Grand Buddha: A massive bronze Buddha statue, one of the largest in the world, located in a scenic area with temples and gardens.
  • Yuantouzhu (Turtle Head Isle): A scenic spot on Taihu Lake, featuring beautiful gardens, pavilions, and panoramic views.
  • Wuxi Ancient Water Town: A well-preserved ancient town with canals, bridges, and traditional architecture.
  • China Dinosaur Park: A popular theme park featuring dinosaur exhibits, rides, and entertainment.

Our Wuxi Tour Packages

We offer a variety of tour packages to suit your interests and schedule:

  • Wuxi Classic Tour (2-3 days): Visit the most popular attractions including Taihu Lake, Lingshan Grand Buddha, and Yuantouzhu.
  • Wuxi Cultural Tour (3-4 days): Explore Wuxi's rich cultural heritage with visits to ancient towns, temples, and museums.
  • Wuxi Nature Tour: Discover the natural beauty of Wuxi with visits to Taihu Lake, scenic parks, and gardens.
