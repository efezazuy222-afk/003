Nanjing Tours

Nanjing, one of China's ancient capitals, is a city steeped in history and culture. With a history dating back over 2,500 years, Nanjing has served as the capital for several Chinese dynasties and is home to numerous historical landmarks and cultural treasures.

Top Attractions

  • Sun Yat-sen Mausoleum: The final resting place of Sun Yat-sen, the founding father of modern China, featuring a grand memorial hall and scenic surroundings.
  • Confucius Temple: A historic temple complex dedicated to Confucius, featuring traditional architecture, shops, and restaurants.
  • Presidential Palace: A former presidential residence and government office, showcasing republican-era architecture and history.
  • Nanjing City Wall: One of the longest city walls in the world, built during the Ming Dynasty.
  • Nanjing Massacre Memorial Hall: A memorial dedicated to the victims of the Nanjing Massacre, serving as a reminder of the city's tragic history.
