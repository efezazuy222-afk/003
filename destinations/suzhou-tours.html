Suzhou Tours

Suzhou, known as the 'Venice of the East', is famous for its classical gardens, canals, and traditional architecture. With a history dating back over 2,500 years, Suzhou is a city of cultural significance and natural beauty.

Top Attractions

  • Humble Administrator's Garden: One of the most famous classical gardens in China, featuring traditional pavilions, ponds, and rockeries.
  • Zhouzhuang Water Town: A well-preserved ancient water town with canals, bridges, and traditional houses.
  • Tiger Hill: A scenic hill with historical sites, including the Yunyan Pagoda and Sword Pond.
  • Suzhou Museum: A modern museum designed by I.M. Pei, showcasing Suzhou's cultural heritage.
  • Shantang Street: A historic street along a canal, lined with traditional shops and restaurants.

Our Suzhou Tour Packages
