Shanghai Tours

Shanghai, China's largest city and financial hub, is a dynamic metropolis where East meets West. Known for its iconic skyline, bustling streets, and rich cultural heritage, Shanghai offers a unique blend of traditional Chinese culture and modern urban life.

Top Attractions

  • The Bund: A historic waterfront area with colonial-era buildings and stunning views of the Pudong skyline.
  • Shanghai Tower: China's tallest building and the second tallest in the world, offering panoramic views from its observation deck.
  • Yu Garden: A classical Chinese garden with pavilions, ponds, and rockeries, dating back to the Ming Dynasty.
  • Nanjing Road: One of the world's busiest shopping streets, lined with department stores, boutiques, and restaurants.
  • French Concession: A charming area with tree-lined streets, colonial architecture, and trendy cafes and shops.

Our Shanghai Tour Packages

We offer a variety of tour packages to suit your interests and schedule:

  • Shanghai Classic Tour (2-3 days): Visit the most popular attractions including the Bund, Shanghai Tower, and Yu Garden.
  • Shanghai Cultural Tour (3-5 days): Explore Shanghai's rich cultural heritage with visits to traditional neighborhoods, museums, and cultural performances.
  • Shanghai Food Tour: Discover Shanghai's diverse culinary scene with guided food tours of local markets and restaurants.
