East China Tours

Our East China tours offer comprehensive exploration of one of China's most fascinating regions. East China is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and vibrant cities. Our tours take you to the most popular destinations in this region, providing a well-rounded experience of East China's unique charm.

Why Choose East China Tours

  • Rich Cultural Heritage: Explore ancient cities, classical gardens, and historical landmarks.
  • Stunning Natural Beauty: Visit scenic spots such as West Lake and Taihu Lake.
  • Vibrant Cities: Experience the modern metropolises of Shanghai and Hangzhou.
  • Convenient Travel: Well-connected destinations making travel between cities efficient.
  • Authentic Cuisine: Sample the delicious local cuisine of East China.

Our East China Tour Packages

  • Classic East China Tour (7-10 days): Visit the most popular destinations including Shanghai, Hangzhou, Suzhou, and Nanjing.
  • Cultural East China Tour (10-14 days): Dive deeper into the cultural heritage of East China with visits to ancient towns, classical gardens, and cultural sites.
  • Scenic East China Tour: Focus on the natural beauty of East China, including West Lake, Taihu Lake, and other scenic areas.
  • Urban East China Tour: Explore the modern cities of East China, including Shanghai, Hangzhou, and Nanjing.

What's Included

  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Comfortable transportation between destinations
  • Accommodation in quality hotels
  • Meals during the tour
  • Entrance fees to attractions
  • Detailed itinerary with sightseeing and free time
  • 24/7 support during your trip

How to Book

To book an East China tour, contact us with your travel dates, group size, interests, and any specific requirements. Our travel experts will create a customized itinerary that meets your needs, ensuring a memorable and enriching experience of East China.
