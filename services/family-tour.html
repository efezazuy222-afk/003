Family Tours

Our family tours are specially designed to create memorable experiences for travelers of all ages. We understand the unique needs of families and have crafted itineraries that balance education, entertainment, and relaxation, ensuring that both children and adults have an enjoyable and enriching travel experience.

Why Choose Family Tours

  • Family-Friendly Activities: Engaging activities suitable for all ages, including interactive museums, theme parks, and cultural experiences.
  • Comfortable Accommodations: Family-friendly hotels with spacious rooms and amenities for children.
  • Flexible Itineraries: Well-paced schedules with adequate rest time and opportunities for free exploration.
  • Educational Experiences: Age-appropriate educational activities that teach children about Chinese culture and history.
  • Family-Focused Guides: Experienced guides who are patient and skilled at engaging with children.

Our Family Tour Packages

  • Family City Tours: Explore China's major cities with activities designed for families, including visits to child-friendly attractions and parks.
  • Family Nature Tours: Discover China's natural beauty with family-friendly outdoor activities, such as hiking, boating, and wildlife watching.
  • Family Cultural Tours: Immerse your family in Chinese culture with hands-on activities, such as calligraphy, traditional craft making, and cooking classes.
  • Family Theme Park Tours: Visit China's exciting theme parks, including Disneyland Shanghai and Chimelong Ocean Kingdom.

What's Included

  • Professional English-speaking guide experienced with families
  • Family-friendly accommodation
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Age-appropriate activities
  • Meals suitable for all ages
  • Entrance fees to attractions
  • Flexible itinerary with rest time

How to Book

To book a family tour, simply contact us with your travel dates, family size, ages of children, and any specific interests or requirements. Our travel experts will create a customized itinerary that caters to your family's needs and preferences, ensuring a memorable and stress-free travel experience.
