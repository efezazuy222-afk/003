Private Tours

Our private tours offer a personalized travel experience tailored to your specific interests, preferences, and schedule. Whether you're looking for a cultural immersion, historical exploration, or natural beauty, our expert guides will create a customized itinerary just for you.

Why Choose Private Tours

  • Personalized Attention: Your dedicated guide will focus solely on your group, providing in-depth information and answering all your questions.
  • Flexible Itineraries: Customize your tour to include the attractions and activities that interest you most.
  • Convenient Schedule: Set your own pace and schedule, allowing for more time at places you love and less time at places that don't interest you.
  • Exclusive Experiences: Access to special experiences not available to regular tour groups.
  • Comfortable Transportation: Private vehicle with a professional driver, ensuring a comfortable and efficient journey.

Our Private Tour Packages

  • Private City Tours: Explore a single city in depth, including its major attractions and hidden gems.
  • Private Multi-City Tours: Visit multiple destinations with a customized itinerary that connects them seamlessly.
  • Private Cultural Tours: Immerse yourself in local culture with visits to traditional villages, markets, and cultural performances.
  • Private Nature Tours: Discover China's natural beauty with guided tours of national parks, scenic areas, and natural wonders.

What's Included

  • Professional English-speaking guide
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Accommodation (optional)
  • Meals (optional)
  • Entrance fees to attractions
  • Customized itinerary

How to Book

To book a private tour, simply contact us with your travel dates, group size, interests, and any specific requirements. Our travel experts will create a customized itinerary and provide you with a detailed quote. Once you confirm, we'll take care of all the arrangements, ensuring a seamless and memorable travel experience.
