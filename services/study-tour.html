Study Tours

Our study tours are designed to provide educational experiences that complement classroom learning. These tours focus on Chinese culture, history, language, and society, offering students and educational institutions a unique opportunity to learn about China firsthand through immersive experiences.

Why Choose Study Tours

  • Educational Value: Structured learning experiences that align with educational objectives.
  • Cultural Immersion: Opportunities to interact with local people and experience daily life in China.
  • Expert Guidance: Knowledgeable guides with educational backgrounds who can provide in-depth information.
  • Hands-on Activities: Interactive workshops and activities that enhance learning.
  • Customizable Itineraries: Programs tailored to specific academic disciplines and learning goals.

Our Study Tour Packages

  • Cultural Study Tours: Focus on Chinese history, art, architecture, and traditional culture.
  • Language Study Tours: Include language classes, cultural exchange activities, and opportunities to practice Chinese with native speakers.
  • Business Study Tours: Explore China's economic development, business practices, and industry trends.
  • Science and Technology Study Tours: Visit research institutions, technology parks, and innovation centers.
  • Environmental Study Tours: Learn about China's environmental challenges and conservation efforts.

What's Included

  • Professional English-speaking guide with educational background
  • Educational materials and resources
  • Accommodation in student-friendly facilities
  • Transportation throughout the tour
  • Entrance fees to educational institutions and attractions
  • Cultural activities and workshops
  • Meals during the tour

How to Book

To book a study tour, contact us with your educational institution's information, group size, academic focus, travel dates, and learning objectives. Our education specialists will design a customized program that meets your specific needs, ensuring a valuable and enriching educational experience for your students.
