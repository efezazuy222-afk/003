Beijing Tours

Beijing, the capital of China, is a city where ancient history meets modern innovation. With over 3,000 years of history, Beijing is home to some of China's most iconic landmarks and cultural treasures.

Top Attractions

  • Great Wall of China: One of the Seven Wonders of the World, the Great Wall offers breathtaking views and a glimpse into China's ancient defensive architecture.
  • Forbidden City: The imperial palace of China's emperors for over 500 years, featuring stunning architecture and historical artifacts.
  • Tiananmen Square: The largest public square in the world, surrounded by important government buildings and historical monuments.
  • Summer Palace: A beautiful imperial garden with lakes, pavilions, and traditional Chinese architecture.
  • Temple of Heaven: A sacred site where emperors prayed for good harvests, featuring intricate designs and spiritual significance.
