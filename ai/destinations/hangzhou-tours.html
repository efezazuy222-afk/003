Hangzhou Tours

Hangzhou, known as 'Heaven on Earth', is a city of stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Famous for its West Lake, traditional Chinese gardens, and silk production, Hangzhou offers a peaceful retreat from the bustling cities of China.

Top Attractions

  • West Lake: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and the most famous attraction in Hangzhou, featuring scenic spots, temples, and pagodas.
  • Lingyin Temple: One of the most important Buddhist temples in China, surrounded by beautiful mountains and ancient trees.
  • Longjing Tea Plantations: Famous for producing China's premium Longjing green tea, offering tea tasting and picking experiences.
  • Leifeng Pagoda: A historic pagoda with panoramic views of West Lake and the surrounding area.
  • Wuzhen Water Town: A charming ancient water town near Hangzhou, featuring traditional architecture and canals.

Our Hangzhou Tour Packages

We offer a variety of tour packages to suit your interests and schedule:

  • Hangzhou Classic Tour (2-3 days): Visit the most popular attractions including West Lake, Lingyin Temple, and Longjing Tea Plantations.
  • Hangzhou Cultural Tour (3-4 days): Explore Hangzhou's rich cultural heritage with visits to traditional gardens, museums, and cultural performances.
  • Hangzhou Water Town Tour: Discover the charming water towns near Hangzhou, including Wuzhen and Xitang.
